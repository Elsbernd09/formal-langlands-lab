# Version 13 Bounded-Plus-Out-of-Range Agreement Packages

Version 13 Phase 96 combines bounded agreement with out-of-range agreement samples.

## Motivation

The universal agreement theorem requires two major ingredients:

1. agreement inside the bounded search range
2. agreement outside the bounded search range

Version 11 gave bounded agreement.

Version 13 Phase 95 gave explicit out-of-range agreement samples.

This phase packages them together.

## For 6

The package contains:

- bounded agreement from 0 through 6
- out-of-range agreement examples for 7, 8, 9, and 10

## For 12

The package contains:

- bounded agreement from 0 through 12
- out-of-range agreement examples for 13, 14, 15, and 16

## Strong Agreement Preparation

The phase also defines a combined preparation package containing both the 6-package and the 12-package.

## Honest Scope

This is still not the final universal theorem over all natural numbers.

It is a stronger preparation layer before the next universal membership attempt.

# Version 13 Expected Out-of-Range Lemmas

Version 13 begins strengthening the expected-Finset side of the universal agreement project.

## Motivation

Version 12 prepared universal membership agreement for the divisor Finsets of 6 and 12.

The missing ingredient is a clean expected-Finset out-of-range theorem.

For 6, the desired theorem is:

`6 < d → d ∉ v10ExpectedDivisorsSix`
